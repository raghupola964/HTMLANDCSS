1. Display Pixels: These are the physical pixels on your screen that display the webpage's content. Tracking Pixels: These are tiny, invisible images (usually 1x1 pixel) embedded in the code of a webpage.   They are used for tracking user behavior and gathering information about website visitors.   Tracking pixels can be used for various purposes, such as: Measuring website traffic and engagement   Tracking conversions (e.g., purchases, form submissions)   Retargeting ads to users who have visited the website   Analyzing user demographics and interests
